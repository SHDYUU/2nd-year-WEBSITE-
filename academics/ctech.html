College of Automation and Control Engineering

BS in Mechatronics Engineering

Aims to equip graduates with the essential skills to design, develop, and integrate intelligent mechanical, electronic, and software systems for automation and robotics applications.

BS in Instrumentation and Control Engineering

Aims to provide graduates with a comprehensive understanding of the design, development, and management of instrumentation and control systems across various industries. This program equips graduates with the technical skills and problem-solving abilities necessary to address complex engineering challenges and drive innovation in automation and process control.

BET major in Mechatronics Engineering Technology

Aims to provide graduates with technological skills in design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware components and systems in modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment.

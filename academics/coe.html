College of Engineering

BS in Electronics Engineering

Designed to train graduates to have an in-depth knowledge on the fundamentals of Electronics Engineering and through understanding of scientific principles, and to practice the profession with high sense of professional responsibility and social awareness.

BS in Mechanical Engineering

Aims to provide graduates with globally competitive skills in mechanical design, steam power and energy conversion, fuel and combustion technologies, heat transfer, material science, manufacturing processes, management, environmental studies, process controls, project research, and the technological impacts on society.

BS in Electrical Engineering

Aims to develop skills in electrical power generation, transmission and distribution, electrical wiring and equipment design, as well as lighting systems of residential, commercial and industrial establishments.

BS in Computer Engineering

Aims to apply engineering principles and methodologies in the analysis, design, implementation, and management of hardware as well as its underlying software and of the integration of both.

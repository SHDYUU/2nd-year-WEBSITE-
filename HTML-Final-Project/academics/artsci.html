College of Engineering Technology

BS in Chemistry

Aims to provide graduates with solid foundations to practice their profession in the field of Chemistry and to contribute to the development of small and medium scale industries relevant to their chosen field.

BET major in Automotive Engineering Technology

Aims to provide graduates with globally competitive skills in mechanical design, steam power and energy conversion, fuel and combustion technologies, heat transfer, material science, manufacturing processes, management, environmental studies, process controls, project research, and the technological impacts on society.

BET major in Chemical Engineering Technology

Structured to support the various industrial operations performing physico-chemical or biochemical synthesis, sale of chemicals, and chemical approaches. The curriculum emphasizes active learning with strong laboratory component and nurtures the holistic development of its students to prepare and encourage them to be highly competent Chemical Technicians.

BET major in Electrical Engineering Technology

Implements and applies the principles of Electrical Engineering that deals with the design, application, installation, manufacturing, operation and maintenance of electrical systems.

BET major in Electronics Engineering Technology

This field explores the principles and applications of electronic devices, analyzes various electronic circuits, and ensures a clear understanding of the function of electronic components. It provides knowledge of passive and active components used in electronic circuits, electronic communications, automatic data processing, and industrial applications. Additionally, it includes training in technical and professional subjects relevant in the electronics technical curriculum and licensure examinations.

BET major in Manufacturing Engineering Technology

Structured to support manufacturing operations with its curriculum strong emphasis on manufacturing, automation, and metal-oriented industries.

BET major in Heating, Ventilating, Air Conditioning Refrigeration Engineering Technology

Designed to provide graduates with specialized expertise in HVACR, including sales, design, maintenance, services, and equipment testing for diverse industrial and commercial applications. The program focuses on creating comfortable, preserved, and safe indoor environments through HVACR systems.

BET in Electromechanical Engineering Technology

Unique program of TUPV that equip graduates with knowledge and skills necessary to apply current methods and technology to the development, design, operation, and management of electro-mechanical systems particularly in industries where automated systems are prevalent.

BET major in Computer Engineering Technology

Aims to provide graduates with technological skills in design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware components and systems in modern computing systems and computer-controlled equipment.

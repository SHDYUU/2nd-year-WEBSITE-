The Technological University of the Philippines â€“ Visayas (TUPV) is a distinguished state university located in Talisay City, Negros Occidental. Established in 1977 as the Visayas Technician Institute (VTI), it became part of the TUP system in 1978 and was renamed TUP Visayas in 1985.

Academic Excellence and Achievements

  • Top Performing School: In April 2025, TUPV achieved a 96.30% passing rate in the Electronics Technician Licensure Examination, surpassing the national average of 81.75%. Graduates Jayvee Fuentebella and Jan Frederick Valenton both secured first place with a rating of 95%.
  • Consistent Board Exam Success: TUPV has a history of producing topnotchers in various licensure exams, including the Electronics Engineer Licensure Examination, where it ranked second among top-performing schools with an 88.16% passing rate in April 2025.

Campus and Programs

  • Main Campus and Extensions: The main campus is situated in Talisay City, with extension campuses in Sagay City and Cadiz City.
  • Academic Offerings: TUPV provides a range of programs, including 3-year technology courses, bachelor's degrees, and master's degrees in technology.

Commitment to Innovation and Sustainability

Technology Conventions: TUPV actively promotes research and innovation through events like its 5th Technology Convention, which showcased Blue, Green, and Circular Economy-related technologies.

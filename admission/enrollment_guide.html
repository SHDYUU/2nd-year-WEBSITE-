To all who passed the admission test and interview:
Please submit your complete enrollment and medical requirements on or before
June 20, 2025.

Enrollment Requirements
  • Academic Records:
    For Senior High School (SHS) Graduates: Original Report Card (Form 138)
    For Transferees: Transcript of Records (TOR) and Certificate of Transfer Credential
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate from PSA
  • Two (2) pieces of 2x2 ID photos (Write full name and course on back)
  • Original Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • SHS Student's Permanent Record / SF10 / Form 137 (if available only)
Medical Requirements
  • Results of the following laboratory tests:
    • Chest X-Ray Result
    • Drug Test Result
    • CBC Test Result
    • Stool Exam Result
    • Urinalysis Result
    • Serum HBsAg (Hepatitis B Surface Antigen) Result
  • Medical Certificate (From a Medical Doctor of your choice)
  • Please photocopy all test results and the medical certificate
